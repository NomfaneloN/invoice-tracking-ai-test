**Ref No used:** PAY4709/2026 (from TC-01)

### Assertions

### Changes Observed
1. **[STEP NAME CHANGE]**: The active step when opening the record after TC-01 is **"Assign Branch Finance Admin To Assign Certifier"**, not "Register and Upload Invoice" as the test plan states. The "Register and Upload Invoice" step was completed in TC-01.
2. **[NAVIGATION CHANGE] Search icon in My Items opens read-only view**: Clicking the search icon in My Items opens `/shesha/workflow?id=...` — a **read-only details page**, not the action form. To reach the workflow action form, navigate to **Inbox** and click the search icon there.
3. **[PAGE TITLE CHANGE] Inbox is titled "Incoming Items"**: The Inbox page heading reads "Incoming Items", not "Inbox".
4. **[NEW COLUMNS] Inbox has additional columns**: Initiator, Action Required, Received Date, Period In Possession, Target Date — not documented in the test plan.
5. **[BEHAVIOR CHANGE] Post-submit navigation from TC-02**: After submitting the "Assign Branch Finance Admin" step, the app redirects to **My Items** (unlike TC-01 which loaded the next step inline).
6. **[CLOSE BUTTON DESTINATION]**: The "Close" button on the workflow action page links to **Inbox** (`/workflows-inbox`), not My Items.
