The auto-authorise step must be LOGIS-branch-specific.
2. **Historical LOGIS records are also stuck at Final Authorise Payment.**
   - PAY5154/2026 — submitted 15/04/2026 (6 days ago), still status "Verified" at Final Authorise Payment.
   - PAY5103/2026 — submitted 14/04/2026 (7 days ago), still status "Certified" at Approve Invoice.
   - PAY5087/2026 (System Administrator as initiator) — also stuck.
   No LOGIS record has successfully completed TC-10 in this QA environment as far back as the inbox shows.
3. **Final Authorise Payment form has no Submit button** (for Thulilem OR for admin). Only "Close" is visible. This step is 100% driven by the BAS Report Import transition, and that transition is broken for LOGIS.
