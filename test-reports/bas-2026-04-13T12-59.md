## Exception Path Behaviour — Confirmed Observations

This E run is the first full execution of the "Send for business related query" exception path. Key findings:

1. **Routing:** Selecting "Send for business related query" at Prepare Voucher routes PAY4982 to the **certifier's** (TaniaSmith's) inbox — not the initiator's (Thulilem's) inbox.
2. **Action name:** Appears in TaniaSmith's inbox as **"Resolve Queries"** (not "Manage Business Related Queries").
3. **Loop-back:** After TaniaSmith submits "Resolve Queries", PAY4982 returns to **Thulilem's** inbox as "Prepare Voucher" — the full Prepare Voucher step is repeated, this time selecting "Verification is complete".
4. **No extra data entry:** The "Resolve Queries" form did not require additional data — just a submit action.
5. **Remainder of workflow:** TC-07 through TC-11 proceed identically to the normal path.
