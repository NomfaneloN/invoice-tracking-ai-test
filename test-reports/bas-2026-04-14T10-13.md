## Recommendations

1. **Dialog hint text bug (High):** Fix the "Send for supplier related query" confirmation dialog hint to correctly read "You selected 'Send for supplier related query'. Please write a query." 2. **Loop-back behaviour documentation:** The post-TC-11 loop-back to "Prepare Voucher" with all selections reset is expected but may surprise users. Consider adding a brief informational message (e.g. "Supplier query resolved — please complete the voucher preparation again") to clarify why the form re-appears blank.
